# Task 2 — Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
Create visualizations using Superstore sales data to convey a compelling business story.

## Tool Used
Tableau Public / Power BI Desktop

## Dataset
Sample Superstore Sales Data (9,994 rows, 21 columns)

## Key Visualizations
1. Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
2. Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
3. Sales by Segment (Donut Chart)
4. Top Sub-Categories (Horizontal Bar)
5. Profit by Region (Map/Bar)

## Key Business Insights
- Technology is the top-selling category ($836K)
- Q4 drives 35% of annual revenue — strong seasonality
- Consumer segment = 51% of total sales
- Central region has lowest profit margin despite decent sales
